# fighting_deck
Small demo I made to check out deckbuilding real time combat and find if it can be funny or it's too hard. The idea resolves around picking which card is best for each situation quickly while dodging enemies and bullets. Although there are only 2 cards in this demo i found a posible fun mechanic, so maybe this turns into a wider game, hope you like it.

## Controls
The controls are simple, and intuitive, and basic, and prepared for a platformer, and there they are:
- A and D for movement
- Space for jumping
- Mouse for aiming and right click for triggering
- Q and E to move between the cards and prepare for triggering them

In the gameScenes (there are 2 "levels" right now), 1 and 2 changes the scene and restarts the game, the scene change is not perfect as it was not a priority but is functional.
